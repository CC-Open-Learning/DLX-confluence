  • When contacting Content Experts to request participants, tell them:

    • How long will it take to run?

    • What is the timeline for performing UAT (start date, end date)?

    • Are participants assigned a specific timeframe to perform the testing?

  • Chosen method for gathering feedback.

    • The participant is presented with a link to a survey at the end of the simulation

      • Participant contact information is provided by the content experts

      • The survey (Microsoft Forms) opens in a new tab

    • The participant completes the survey

    • When they close the survey they have completed the user testing task

  • Link to the tool used to get feedback.

    • Machine Shop UAT Survey (MS Forms)

  • Estimated time for completion of the UAT:

    • Time to complete is 30-40 minutes

      • The minimum run time is 25 minutes for the simulation

      • Survey time is 5 minutes
