VARLab : Creator Dashboard

Created by Hannah Schmitting (Unlicensed), last modified on Mar 28, 2024

The Creator Dashboard serves as (one of several) places where you can access information about your games and reach other areas of the site.

In the Projects tab, you’ll find a list of all of your current projects both published and unpublished.

This portion of the Dashboard’s main page offers a brief summary of all of your games' combined analytics. Selecting “View more” will bring you to the Analytics tab of your dashboard. Selecting the Analytics tab from the top of the page will have the same result.

“Views” offers a more substantial breakdown of the number of views your games have received over time.

“Downloads”… the same but for game downloads.

Also under the Analytics tab is the Referrers section. This allows you to keep track of the external sources through which people are accessing your games and how often those links are used.
