VARLab : GMP - Knowledge Check Q&A

Created by Stacey Dineen (Unlicensed) on Nov 04, 2022

After each learning segment, learners are given a simple multiple-choice question to confirm what they have learned. This page contains all of those knowledge check questions and answers, in order.

Segment 1 - Introduction to GMP’s

What does GMP stand for?

  • Great Meat Products

  • Good Manufacturing Practices

  • Good Meat Packaging

  • Good Management Practice

Segment 1 - Food Safety Hazards

Biological contamination

Which of the following can cause biological contamination?

  • Cuts and injuries

  • Spitting in the workplace

  • Working when sick

  • All answers are correct

Chemical contamination

Which of the following is important about chemicals in the workplace?

  • Must be properly labelled

  • Must be used by trained personnel

  • Must be stored correctly

  • All answers are correct

Physical contamination

Which of the following are examples of physical contamination?

  • Cuts while working

  • Incorrectly stored chemicals

  • Jewelry falling into food

  • All answers are correct

Importance of GMPs

Why are GMPs important?

  • Consumer safety

  • Food safety

  • Employee safety

  • All answers are correct

Segment 2 - Personnel Practices

Dress Code

What should be worn to work?

  • Wear comfortable clothes

  • Clean street clothes are acceptable

  • Dress as specified in facility SOP

  • None of the answers are correct

Personal Protective Equipment

Why is PPE important in food production?

  • Keeps you and the product safe

  • PPE is optional

  • To protect your fake nails

  • None of the answers are correct

Hygiene

Which of the following examples are important for workplace hygiene?

  • Wearing high quality fake nails

  • Wearing stylish clothing

  • Showering before coming to work

  • None of the answers are correct

Healthy Safe Workplace

What should you do if you are feeling sick?

  • Come to work with a facemask

  • Come to work as usual

  • Stay at home and consult a supervisor

  • All answers are correct

  • When very hungry
