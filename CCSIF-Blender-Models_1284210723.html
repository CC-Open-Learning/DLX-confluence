VARLab : CCSIF Blender Models

Created by User 328bc on Aug 14, 2024

This article is for information about the newly created Blender Models.

Limitations

CCSIF is funded differently from the rest of VARLabs which means we don’t have access to their art team. This means that we are highly encouraged to make our own assets. All of the models that were made during this project were taken from the previous trucking inspection and were altered to fit our needs. Our blender knowledge is either nonexistent or extremely limited, meaning that the models we created have some known issues.

Standards

All models created need to follow the artistic naming conventions in addition to being uploaded to the sharepoint.

Sharepoint: https://stuconestogacon.sharepoint.com/:f:/s/VARLab/EgSv06yV23NMmQUBSw7EiccBz1-WGaCqkVj64mie-bgYgA?e=ZuCBEc

Naming Conventions: On Universal Naming Conventions

Common Issues

  • Bad Scale: The model is has the wrong scale and is either too large or too small.

  • Misaligned Transform Point: The model’s point of transform is far away from the model

  • Missing Material: The model is missing a material and is the default white.

Most bad states use decals but WebGL doesn’t work with dented decals which required us to make dented models.
