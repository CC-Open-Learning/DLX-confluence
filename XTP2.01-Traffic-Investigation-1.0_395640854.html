Students have the opportunity to apply procedures for investigating a car crash and conducting an analysis on the crash scene to determine what charges (if any) should be laid against the driver(s) involved according to their knowledge and understanding of the Ontario Highway Traffic Act. (This is an early version – a more complex version has been developed for both flatscreen and VR Headset play)
