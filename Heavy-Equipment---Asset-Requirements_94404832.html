    Modelling Requirements

    Jira Issue

    1

    Caterpillar 416F Backhoe Exterior

    The scenario begins with a user approaching the Caterpillar 416F Backhoe and conducting a visual inspection of the cab.

    Doors to be modelled separately (see below). The engine compartment lid shall also be modelled separately (see below).

    Requires (1) model, and (2) textures. The good texture presents good normal exterior paint, whereas a bad texture is a rusted machine.

    https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/CE-484
