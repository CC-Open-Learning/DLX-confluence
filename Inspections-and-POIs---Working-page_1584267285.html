DaVinci v2 Curved Magnum Cartridges

https://www.mapletattoosupply.com/products/da-vinci-v2-curved-magnum-cartridges

Available gauge: Bugpin(10) or Regular(12)

Sizes 05CM up to 27CM (odd numbers only 5, 7, 9, 11). The number appears to correspond to the number of needles, see photos.

CM stands for Curved Magnum, not centimetres

“Magnum” cartridges appear identical to Curved Magnum cartridges, I cannot see any difference

4 Different States for Cartridges:

  • in package

  • fouled or compromised packaging

  • installed in the tattoo machine (not packaged)

  • loose (in wastebasket or left somewhere on a counter where it does not belong)

Must be laid out ready to use in the sterile packaging - tray is laid out with all pieces intact. Each element would be individually inspectable.

Assembling the tattoo gun should happen in front of the client - cords covered, good infection control practices.

We need to see pre and post assembly
