(Guided by Avatar Digital Teaching Assistant on 360 photos of the restaurant environment)

This series of 13 modules introduces students to the equipment and kitchen of the Bloom Restaurant at Waterloo campus. Students are guided through the Bloom environment and become familar with the equipment, operations and safety procedures of the kitchen by Fanny, an avatar who acts as their guide through the digital experience.  (Not currently in use – was adapted as a pandemic measure).
