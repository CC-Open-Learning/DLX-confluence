Trucking Inspection Overall Order

The overall inspection order that was presented in the video inspection from our content expert is recorded in https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/VAR/pages/456851518/Trucking+FRS+-+DLS+Level#Order-of-Inspection-Tasks---Based-on-Live-Inspection-Demo-Reference-Video-from-Way-Freight. This is not a requirement to follow and we can propose our own inspection order that makes the most sense for the trucking sim.

The plan is to make guided mode linear, and have the student on rails moving from one inspection/POI to another only progressing if they press the “Next Inspection” button and answer all inspectables at the current POI correctly. However, in advanced mode the student will be the one driving the sim and have the option to select where they want to navigate to, what to inspect, have the option to go back to change answers if they desire, or skip sections entirely.

Guided Mode

The only proposed section that is complete currently are the wheels. Anything else is yet to even be implemented.

  2. DS Steer Tire

  3. DS Rear Truck Tires x 2

  4. DS Trailer Tires X 2

  5. Repeat steps 2-4 for PS wheels

  6. TBD

Guided Mode - Wheel Inspection Order

The wheel inspection is broken up into five steps/POIs for the student. Each wheel inspection in the overall order of the sim will have the same steps/order depending on which tire is being inspected.

  1. Tire tread is inspected

    1. Debris is also inspected in the same step here only if it is a set of double tires

  2. Rims and tire side wall are inspected

  3. Lug nuts are inspected

  4. Tire pressure is inspected

  5. Mud flaps for the wheels are inspected only if they are present at that set of wheels
