VARLab : Side Quests

Created by Colm Ambler (Unlicensed) on Aug 03, 2022

Name: Avi's OneCard

ID: cardQuest

Objective: The player must find Avi's ONECard somewhere in the school and return it to him.

Events:

  1. Talk with Avi in the cafeteria.

  2. Find Avi in The Pit.

  3. Find Avi in The Agora.

  4. Find Avi in 2F12.

  5. Find Avi's ONECard in 2F12 and return it to him.

Alternative Events:

  1. Find Avi's ONECard before talking with him in the cafeteria.

  2. Find the ONECard after talking with Avi and then returning it to him either when he is in The Pit or the Agora.

Major Locations:

  1. Cafeteria, B Wing 1F.

  2. The Pit, A Wing 1F.

  3. The Agora, F Wing 2F.

  4. 2F12. F Wing 2F.

Learning Outcomes:

  • Learn what you should do when you lose your ONECard (freeze the account and order a new one online).

  • Learn about The Pit, the Agora (they are referenced as such in the quest), and one of the classrooms (re-iterate how to read class names).

Characters:

  • Condor

  • Ezra

  • Avi

  • Jemima

Name: Barty's Cane

ID: bartyQuest

Objective: The player must use the elevator to reach the second floor and get Barty's walking cane from their locker.

Events:

  1. Talk with Barty in E Wing First Floor.

  2. Walk towards the elevator and go up to the second floor.

  3. Walk towards Barty's locker and begin the final scene.

Alternative Events:

  1. If the player attempts to leave E Wing in any capacity, go into the CozE corner, or tries to walk up steps, a short exchange will happen and the condor will take a step back.

Major Locations:

  1. E Wing, 1F.

  2. E Wing, 2F.

Learning Outcomes:

  • Teach the player about the elevators on Doon campus and how you can use them.

Characters:

  • Condor

  • Ezra

  • Barty

Name: Getting the band Together

ID: bandQuest

Objective: The player must find 2 band members for Seamus, an aspiring musician. Once they do, they must meet up with Seamus in the library.

Events:

  1. Talk with Seamus to initiate the quest.

  2. Talk with Ruth in the CozE corner.

  3. Talk with Jemima after completing Avi's ONECard quest.

  4. Head back towards C Wing where the Condor will get a text from Seamus to head to the library.

  5. Meet with the band in the library.

Major Locations:

  1. C1.

  2. B1.

  3. E1.

  4. Library.

  5. NewWorkRoom.

Learning Outcomes:

  • Simple wayfinding through the campus.

  • Using and booking the workrooms within the library and where they are located.

Characters:

  • Condor

  • Ezra

  • Sheamus

  • Ruth

  • Jemima

Name: The VARLab

ID: vQuest

Objective: The Condor must help the VARLab fix their simulations by going into cyberspace and fixing the objects.

Events:

  1. Talk with Santiago, a VARLab ambassador, in The Pit.

  2. Head to 1E15 and talk with Zed, another VARLab ambassador.

  3. Offer to help them with their simulation problems.

  4. Take each of the 6 items in cyberspace and place them in the correct simulations.

Major Locations:

  1. A1(The Pit).

  2. 1E15.

  3. Cyberspace.

Learning Outcomes:

  • Promote the VARLab and the opportunities and what it provides.

  • Works as an example for sponsored content.

Characters:

  • Condor

  • Ezra

  • Santiago

  • Zed

Name: The Clay Statues

ID: clayQuest

Objective: The player must solve the mystery of the clay statues that are appearing above certain lockers in the school.

Events:

  1. Interact with the clay statue on B Wing's first floor.

  2. Find the owner, Jillian, in the CozE Corner.

  3. Find the second clay statue in A Wing, second floor.

  4. Return to Jillian in the CozE corner.

  5. Find the third clay statue on E Wing's third floor.

  6. Return to the locker next to the first clay statue.

Major Locations:

  1. B1.

  2. E1 (CozE Corner).

  3. A2.

  4. E3.

Learning Objectives:

  • Simple wayfinding through exploration of the campus.

Characters:

  • Condor

  • Ezra

  • Jillian

  • Tim
