GENERAL RELEASE

Post UAT Stakeholder Meeting 12 NOV 2021

Post UAT Stakeholder Meeting 12 NOV 2021

Participants: Lisa Trimble, Adam Ziegler, Stacey Dineen

  • New build of Traffic sim received 14 Oct 2021 from XPertVR - functional testing was successful. Test Notes are in SharePoint. The new measuring tool was found to be functional and user-friendly enough to bypass the complaints that were reported previously.

  • Shared and demonstrated the new measuring tool, Adam had already had a chance to play with it. He didn’t love it, still found the process of measuring to be tedious (user has to walk around to finish a longer measurement, no option to make measurements from a bird’s eye view of the scene) but he agreed that it is much more usable than the previous tool. Still not great, but he pointed out that instructors always have the option of taking the measurement tool out of the critical path by simply giving the length of deceleration marks in the assignment, for example

  • Stacey proposed another round of UAT on the basis that the measurement tool had been such a blocking factor in the first round of UAT that much of the simulation was not tested as a result. Lisa agreed. However, with further discussion, it was proposed that we simply go to General Release this term and open the sim up to all current students (at the instructor’s discretion). Functional testing shows very low risk for any major bugs, and we will get better feedback from students when they have unlimited time to use the simulation. In the event that anyone does find a critical issue, we can consider a re-release at that time. For now, it is more useful to us in the hands of students than it is in the lab.

  • All stakeholders agreed to General Release. Stacey proposed release to VLT on Friday 12 Nov 2021.

  • Chosen method for gathering feedback.

    • We will provide a link to the survey on eConestoga, placed after the simulation. Participants should complete it after having used the simulation for 30-45 minutes

  • Link to the tool used to get feedback.

    • Traffic Investigation UAT Feedback Survey

  • Estimated time for completion of the UAT: Participants will be asked to spend 30 - 45 minutes trying the different scenarios in the simulation
