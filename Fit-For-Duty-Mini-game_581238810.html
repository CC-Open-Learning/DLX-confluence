VARLab : Fit For Duty Mini-game

Created by Leo Bunting, last modified by Carolina Naoum Junqueira on Dec 15, 2022

The title will be updated when an appropriate mini-game title is decided on.

The mini-game section of the Fit For Duty segment, like the other mini-games in the Shop Safety Orientation DLS, uses the Drag and Drop interactive learning design.

The mini-game uses a randomized pool of thought and speech bubbles, each with pictures and text respectively, describing and showing things a student should or shouldn’t do before heading to the shop for the day. The player is shown two bubbles at a time and must drag and drop each bubble into either the “DO” or “DON’T” drop boxes. When a bubble is dropped into the correct dropbox, it is removed from the pool of answers. When a bubble is dropped into the incorrect dropbox, it is pushed to the bottom of the list of answers to be shown again later. When all bubbles are placed in their correct dropboxes, the game finishes.
