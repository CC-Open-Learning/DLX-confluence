Created by Sana Javeed, last modified on Jun 24, 2024

Date: April 10, 2024 TO April 19, 2024

This report contains details of the field research conducted with students online for testing the Ambulance and Paramedicine DLX with 3 students.

A survey was also shared providing valuable insights into their experiences and perceptions of the simulation. This report aims to summarize and analyze the feedback, highlighting key areas of improvement and positive aspects.

User flow

From the data, the users in the usability test were able to

  • Explore the Ambulance compartments (Explore mode)

  • Play one scenario of the seek and find I

Positive Aspects¬†

Reports from three users highlight the ambulance experience as a fun and interactive journey, praising its excellent detail and user-friendly interface with no lags.

More positive aspects on the DLX:

  • All 3 users had clear expectations through existence of the levels for exploration and testing knowledge. ( eg: explore, seek and find I, II, III)

  • Written prompts being concise and well-organized attributed towards the ease of completing tasks in the DLX.

  • 2/3 users provided positive feedback on the camera speed and the ability to navigate around the Ambulance.

Some Quotes from the users on the look and feel of the simulation:
