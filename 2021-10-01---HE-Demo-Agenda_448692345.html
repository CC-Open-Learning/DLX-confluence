Notes and Feedback

  • We can demo the steering wheel in the next demo
  • Adding camera POV close to the point of inspection
  • Texturing for the cab interior will be completed soon
  • Make a colour change to indicate leaking hydraulics. The texture change is not quite obvious enough
  • rust is not an issue, show a crack on the arms as a damaged state.
  • no need to show deformation of the arms, that is extremely rare
  • Dirty windows can be just one window, the important thing is for students to learn to check the windows. High realism is not as important
  • Keep the on screen messages as a hint for learners to identify loose wheel nuts vs tight - animation and sound are still a little subtle
  • We will get video for engine start - how this looks inside the cab, gauges moving etc
  • Add universal oil, hydraulic symbols to the engine
  • For very small objects, we can show the oil symbol onscreen when mouse over
  • Paul will send photos
  • Jordan will capture audio of the engine running and tires on the pavement
