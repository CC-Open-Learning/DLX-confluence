VARLab : Nov 6 - AMB Testing Blitz

Created by Cam Turner, last modified on Nov 05, 2024

Testing Instructions:

  • NOTE 1: We have deleted all save files in VARLab-DEV1 for AMB. If you load the sim for this testing and already have progress shown, please contact us before proceeding.

  • NOTE 2: In all cases, you are to adopt the persona of a benign student who is honestly trying to complete their assigned homework. We will conduct an anarchy hostile test during CTT on Nov 8.

  • All testers are assigned into two groups; either full-screen mode or windowed mode. Please ensure you do your testing in the correct mode. If in full-screen mode, please also note the monitor size.

    • If you are designated “Small Monitor” then please use the smallest you own (ideally a 13-15” laptop monitor).

    • If you are designated “Larger Monitor” then use something at least 19” or bigger please.

  • Explore Mode Testing: Begin by achieving 100% on explore mode. This should take about 10-20 minutes max.

    • Confirm that no pockets/compartments/objects are blocked by the on-screen menus in default camera positions.

    • Confirm that items are labeled correctly in the progress panel, on the screen, and in the popup. This is a quick glance test. We will audit exact spelling. You’re just double checking that we don’t have something labelled “Banana” when it’s clearly “Airplane”.

    • Confirm that detail images match the label and model shown.

    • Confirm that when done and you log out (combine with testing below), when you return your progress of 100% is saved.

  • Semester 3 Scenario Content Testing: Everyone is assigned two different Semester 3 scenarios below (since Semesters 1 and 2 have had extensive testing already). For each scenario, you’ll likely need at least 30 minutes to complete the 5 tests below.
    For each of these you need to:

    • Test 1 - Play them completely in Beginner mode. 100% completion with the ending success message.

    • Test 2 - Play them slowly in Intermediate mode. 100% completion with a tiny bit of time left on the clock (less than 30 seconds remaining). This will set a “Personal Best”

    • Then log out of eConestoga, close the browser entirely, reboot the computer (please!)

    • Log back in to eConestoga, open the sim, and confirm that Beginner still shows complete and that your intermediate personal best is saved correctly.

    • Test 3 - Now beat your personal best time and confirm that it’s saved correctly by closing the browser tab and reopening it (no need to log out or reboot this time).

    • Test 4 - Almost done. Now, using the CHEAT SHEET, complete the same scenario in advanced mode using only the expected equipment (no alternatives).

    • Test 5 - Lastly, using the CHEAT SHEET, complete the same scenario in advanced mode using as many alternatives as you can.

  • Advanced Mode “Alternatives” Testing: Everyone is also assigned a single scenario from Semester 1 or 2 that is for ADVANCED MODE only, and we have very specific test requirements:

    • You should submit a list that has ONLY the alternatives provided – but also ALL of the alternatives – in the CHEAT SHEET. It will be an incomplete list of less than 10 items. On the results screen you should see:

      • Correct for the first alternative from each group.

      • Incorrect for the second ( & third, fourth etc) alternative for each group.

      • “Missing” for a bunch of items that are not mentioned at all in either the Equipment or Alternatives list.

      • IMPORTANT: You should NOT see any of the items from the equipment column (first column) mentioned in ANY of the results columns. Why? Alternatives were provided to those items so they are not missing.

Cool? Overall this should take about 75-90 minutes unless you are distracted or interrupted.
