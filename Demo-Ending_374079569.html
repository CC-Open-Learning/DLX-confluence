VARLab : Demo Ending

Created by Hannah Schmitting (Unlicensed), last modified on Sept 08, 2021

(Asmee) Well, we all have our books, right?

(Condor) We do.

(Asmee) And we have each other!

(Condor) I guess so?

(Ezra) Where… where are you going with this?

(Asmee) Well, since Charlie’s not here to do it, one of us has to close out the demo.

(Ezra) What does that have to do with “we have each other”?

(Asmee) The moral is friendship, right? We’ve known each other for about half an hour, that counts.

(Condor) I think the pond would be a better place for the friendship ending than the bookstore.

(Ezra) I think so too, but the school doesn’t have a pond.

(Asmee) Yet.

(Condor) Besides, I think the moral is knowing how to get around the school.

(Ezra) Is that something that can be a moral?

(Asmee) Okay, so our moral is “getting around the school with the power of friendship!”

(Condor) And the power of asking for directions.

(Ezra) I would like some directions, please.

(Condor) East.

(Asmee) Sounds good to me!

[All head east off screen, fade out]
