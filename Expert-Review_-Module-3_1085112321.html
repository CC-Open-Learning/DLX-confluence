Issue Title

Issues (also add screenshots, if any)

Recommendation

1

Accessibility Issues- Sound controls ON/OFF

People who cannot rely on color will not be able to understand whether the controls are meant to indicate ON/OFF

Accessibility Issues

Provide Text like OFF or On next to the control hence supplementing right information.

Hence in the snapshot provided : Narrator witll have ON next to the control and Sound effects will have OFF next to the control

2

Controls panel when DLX opened NOT in full-screen mode

Persistant issue from Module 2a, module 2b. The interaction with the “back” button is very minimal and restrictive when played in NON-fullscreen mode

Resolution fixes

3

Close proximity of the “Start” and “ Go back” buttons

Place the buttons in a way that start is associated with the field

4

Clicking “i” on hover - not intuitive

on hover on the hammer and clicking “i” is not intuitive and requires recollection of commands

a small message at the bottom what to click.

and selecting the hammer and clicking “i” is more intuitive in inspecting the equipment

When clicking “i” there should be a prompt to use “ i” to exit inspecting the tool, since it avoids the cognitive overload on the user to remember theh commands or what to do next

5

Pivotal Rotation of equipment

Pivotal rotation of the hammer feels weird to examine..

6

Does not support individuals with Red-green color blindness

Currently a highlight of the hammer looks like this

7

Equipment seems to be cut off on the top when picking it up

8

Overhead instructions difficult to view

It was not obvious that there were instructions to look at. Does not seem user friendly to walk back to look up.

The spatial instructions could be placed at the user’s eye level
