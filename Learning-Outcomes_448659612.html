VARLab : Learning Outcomes

Created by Hannah Schmitting (Unlicensed), last modified on Oct 01, 2021

This is a living document that outlines the intended learning outcomes of the Campus RPG. Items on this document are subject to change as decisions in the development of the game are made.

  • Bus Sequence
  • Wayfinding (Main) Quest
  • Winter Quest
  • ONE Card Quest
  • Elevator Quest

Bus Sequence

Students will:

  • Know to vacate the front seats of the bus when someone who requires more space or more accommodation enters the bus

  • Know that the front seats of the bus fold up for the purpose of accommodation

  • Understand that when someone requires the ramp to be lowered to enter and exit the bus, that person enters and exits the bus first

Wayfinding (Main) Quest

Students will:

  • Be able to navigate around to the most notable areas of Conestoga College’s Doon campus

  • Become familiar with the Doon campus layout before arriving at the school

  • Learn the basics about what Student Engagement has to offer

Winter Quest

Students will:

  • Understand the inherent dangers of the Canadian winter season

  • Know what equipment you’ll need to keep yourself warm and safe

  • Understand how the room numbering system at the Doon campus works

ONE Card Quest

Students will:

  • Know how to replace their ONE Card if it is lost

  • Learn that a ONE Card can be used as a payment method around campus

  • Discover new locations around campus in their search for Avi’s ONE Card

Elevator Quest

Students will:

  • Learn about the existence of elevators on Doon campus and where to find them

  • Understand that not all disabilities are visible or immediately apparent
