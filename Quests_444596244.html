Wayfinding Quest

*As this is the largest and most detailed quest in the game, this section will be divided into chunks.

Overview

The player plays as a Condor finally attending Conestoga College. As they arrive, they find a student whose having difficulty adjusting to the new environment. The two become friends and search for the library. After they print off a document and hand it to the security officer, they get hungry and decide to grab something to eat. Their journey takes them to the CozE corner, where they order some pizza. After talking about Student Engagement and both of them not really understanding what it’s for, they decide to visit the office. Once they arrive, they meet Asmee, another student. She mentions that she needs to pick up books at the bookstore. All three of them decide to head to pick up their books. Having picked their books up, the three reflect on their journeys and the game ends.

Tutorial

As the game begins, the player is put into a small room where they will learn how to move and interact with the environment. It begins with an autorun event. When the player enters the second room, another autorun event plays. At the end of each event, a self switch is triggered to stop an infinite loop from happening. After talking with the single character, the event TutorialComplete is turned ON and the player can leave through the door.

Ezra + Library

As the player enters the school, they are immediately greeted with Ezra circling around very close to them. The player is unable to move to a different map unless Ezra is a part of their party. When talking with Ezra, the player will be given the option to name the Condor. They will have up to 8 characters to name themselves (due to RPG Maker’s text going offscreen if the name is longer). Afterward, Ezra joins the player’s party. Because Ezra is in the player’s party, they will be able to go to B Building 2F.

Once they head up a couple spaces (or go around) an autorun event will play when touched pointing the player towards the help desk. The help desk has a self switch that is triggered after the first conversation. The player is able to ask the help desk for directions on all the key areas in the wayfinding quest.

Once the player goes into the library, they will enter an autorun event. This autorun event moves the player towards a desk and turns on a self switch when finished. The player interacts with a printer that triggers the Printer variable to = 1. When it = 1, there is new dialogue, and when it = 2, it has even newer dialogue.

After leaving the library, the player talks with the security guard. At the end, the Printer variable = 2 and CafQuest = 1.

Caf + CozE Corner

As the player moves forward towards any entrance of the cafeteria (from the stairs, A Wing or the way to C Wing) they will walk into a region where CafQuest = 2 and a self-switch is triggered.

The Condor and Ezra head towards the CozE Corner. Once they enter, a cutscene will begin that changes slightly depending on where the player enters the CozE corner from. This cutscene combines events and regions. At the entrance are 4 regions (labelled 1, 2, 3, and 4), behind each of them is an event labelled EzraDummy(x). These events are empty until the event related to the regions changes it’s image depending on where the player is. At the same time, it removes Ezra from the party to make this EzraDummy the only instance of Ezra in the scene. Whichever EzraDummy is active walks off screen and teleports to a spot off the immediate screen. Each event page here signals one of the four EzraDummies. To do this to the specific EzraDummy and not the other three, there are four switches related to each respectively. This is done to have control over a single instance of Ezra and cancel the other three. At the end, CafQuest = 3.

Because CafQuest is 3, another event related to different regions is active, this time stopping the player from exiting the CozE corner without Ezra. The player walks over to where Pizza Pizza is in real life and is given the choices of purchasing Pizza or Pizza with Pineapple. Either way, an autorun event will play as soon as it detects either item in the player’s inventory. It turns the variable CafQuest to 4, and the switch PizzaObtained on. As a result, the EzraDummy(x) is erased and a new Ezra arrives at the entrance to the room with Pizza Pizza. When the player exist the room, the new Ezra will approach them and the two will walk toward a table. Near the end, the new Ezra event walks towards the player and Ezra is added to the party again. The image for the Ezra event is turned to nothing, all of the EzraDummy(x) switches are turned off (just in case any of them are active) PizzaEaten is turned ON, and Pizza and Pizza with Pineapple are subtracted by 1000 each from the player’s inventory. This is because the switch PizzaObtained is on if Pizza is in the player’s inventory, and because there is no way to determine how many of each the player purchased without limiting them more a ludicrous number is used. Finally, the event is erased and the player is able to leave the CozE corner.

Student Engagement and Bookstore

With PizzaEaten ON, the player is able to enter the Student Engagement office. On entry, an autorun event will play and a similar trick to the CozE corner scene is used. At the beginning of the event, Ezra is removed from the party. Behind where the player spawns is an EzraDummy event that will follow the player’s movements. This is done because later, Ezra will deviate from the player. The two of them will move, talk and the Asmee event will walk away towards the exit. After some more dialogue, both the Condor and Ezra walk towards the exit to where Asmee is. More dialogue happens before both Ezra and Asmee are added to the player’s party.

With Asmee in the party, the player can enter the bookstore. When they enter and talk with the cashier, they will be given a simple list of choices between Asmee’s books and the Condor’s. There are four event pages with this event that all check whether the player has the Textbook item or the AsmeeBooks switch is ON, or both.

Ending Cutscene

As the player has both AsmeeBooks ON and the item Textbook in their inventory, the final cutscene will automatically play. The player moves a few spaces to the right before the switch CutsceneDummies is turned ON. This switch is used to trigger the two dummy events in the scene for both Ezra and Asmee. At the same time, Ezra and Asmee are removed from the party. After some dialogue, the two dummy events move towards the door and their opacity is reduced to 0. A balloon icon appears above the Condor and the player is transferred to the Ending map. The ending map has an autorun event that plays with dialogue that ends with the player going back to the main menu.

One Card Quest

Overview

A student named Avi in the cafeteria has lost his OneCard. The player can can choose to help him. If they do, he will leave to the first of three locations while his friend, Jemima, stays behind in the cafeteria. The player can find Avi in three different locations, each leading to the next. Eventually, the player will find the card inside his classroom. At which point, they will give him the card, he will thank them and return to the cafeteria.

Variables Used

  • OneCardQuest

Switches Used

  • AviDirection

Items

  • Avi’s One Card

Maps

  • B Wing F1

  • A Wing F1

  • F Wing F2

  • 2F47

Added Actors

  • None

Initiation

The player encounters Avi in the cafeteria. If they talk with him or Jemima, they will learn that his OneCard is missing and they will be given the option to help or wish him luck. If the player wishes him luck, the variable OneCardQuest will change to 1, so that there will be different dialogue when they talk with him again.

If the player offers to help, the switch AviDirection turns on. This is because on the first and second event page (OneCardQuest = 0 or 1) Avi’s event is set to direction fix so that he won’t turn towards the player when speaking to him. This must be changed when he starts moving otherwise he will keep facing the same direction as he walks away.

After Avi walks away, the event is pushed to an area the player cannot access. The same effect could alternatively be done via making Avi’s image disappear but either works.

Quest

Avi moves from one location to the next, only when the player finds and speaks to him. He moves from the pit in A wing, to the Agora in F wing then finally his class in F wing. The OneCard is in the classroom.

When the player encounters Avi in both the Pit and Agora, the logic is the same. After speaking with him, a self switch is triggered that begins his movement. This new event page, more importantly, changed through to on. This allows the event to move through other events and the player, otherwise Avi gets stuck if the player is in his way. At the end of meeting Avi in the pit, OneCardQuest changes to = 3. After talking with him in the agora, it changes to 4.

Investigating the chest in 2F47 reveals the OneCard inside, changing the variable OneCardQuest to 5 and adding Avi’s One Card to the player’s inventory.

Ending

There are multiple locations the quest can end in. As long as the variable OneCardQuest is 5 and Avi’s One Card is in the player’s inventory, they can give the card to Avi at any location. After talking with Avi, OneCardQuest changes to 6 and the item Avi’s One Card is removed from the player’s inventory. The logic for Avi’s exit is the exact same as when he moves from one location to the next. Avi will be back at the cafeteria and both he and Jemima have different dialogue if you talk with them.

Elevator Quest

Overview

As the player reaches the E wing on the first floor, they’ll come across Barty, seemingly having collapsed near some stairs. If the player interacts with him, Barty will let them know that he usually has a cane with him to help him walk, but it’s currently on the second floor of the school. He reccomends taking the elevator to reach the second floor and asks for The Condor’s help. The characters take the elevator up to the second floor and walk towards Barty’s locker. When they do, he grabs his cane and leaves on his merry way.

Variables Used

  • ElevatorQuest

  • CafQuest

Switches Used

  • None

Items

  • None

Maps

  • E Wing 1F

  • E Wing 2F

Added Actors

  • Barty

Initiation

The player will find Barty lying on the floor of E Wing 1F. They can talk to Barty earlier in the library, but he’ll disappear from there when the variable CafQuest = 1. This quest is initiated when CafQuest = 2. The player does not a choice to step aside for this quest and must help him when they initiate the quest. At the end of the dialogue exchange, Barty will join the party and ElevatorQuest will = 1.

Quest

Once the player is back in control, they can only use the elevator. All exits out of E Wing are blocked because Elevator Quest = 1. The player also cannot enter the CozE corner. An event will play when the player in in the four spots in front of the elevator. At this point, the game will point towards it and the player will need to interact with it. ElevatorQuest will increase to 2. When they do, they will be given only one choice, to go up to the second floor. When the player arrives at the second floor, an autorun event plays telling them to head towards Barty’s locker. ElevatorQuest increases to 3. Similarly to the first floor, the player cannot leave this map. They cannot even use the elevator again.

Ending

There are events all around Barty’s locker that trigger when the player touches them. They all move the characters before removing Barty and Ezra. They talk and eventually Barty leaves. Ezra joins the party once more, and the ElevatorQuest variable changes to 4. This allows for all of the elevators in the school to be accessed.

Clay Statue Quest

Overview

In the halls of B Wing’s classrooms on the first floor is a strange clay statue. This statue belongs to someone but the Condor and Ezra need to find out who. Their search leads them to a student Jillian in the CozE corner. The Condor gives them the clay statue, but Ezra notices a strange inscription on the bottom, reading 2A. After searching the halls of 2A, the duo finds another clay statue. They give it o Jillian again who explains that the first statue wasn’t hers. She mentions that her friend, Tim, had an interest in the real clay statue she made. They get the first statue back and notice the inscription that says 3. On the third floor, they find a third statue and realize that all three of them have numbers on them. They believe it must be referencing a locker’s combination and surmise that it must be Tim’s. Going back to Tim’s locker, they try the combination to find that the locker opens. Suddenly, Tim arrives and exclaims his birthday wishes to his friend, only to realize that The Condor and Ezra are not Jillian. Tim explains that he set up the clay statues as hints for Jillian to find, but unfortunately, she didn’t find them. The Condor and Ezra comfort him as they head towards Jillian to give her the gift that was inside the locker. Everyone thanks each other, the end.

Initiation

The player comes across a clay statue in the halls of B Wing 1F. There is nothing to trigger the statue’s appearance, it’s always there. When the player interacts with it, they will be given the option to take it. If they do, The item Strange Clay Statue gets added to the player’s inventory. Afterward, the image on the event “TimDummy” changes its image to that of Tim. The camera scrolls over to see him walk away off screen. The camera pans back to the Condor and Ezra, who talk a bit more before the variable Clay Statue Questline = 1.

Quest

*As this side quest is larger than the other two, we’ll break it up by each clay statue

Clay Statue 1

When the Clay Statue Questline = 1, the player will be able to ask certain characters about the statue. In B Wing 1F, the only person they can ask is the janitor (who before, when Clay Statue Questline was 0, would make reference to the statue). The only other people you can talk about the clay statue to are all in the CozE corner. There are four characters in there, one of them being Jillian. After asking Jillian about the statue, she explains that the statue is hers and the Condor offers it back to her. The Clay Statue Questline = 2 and Strange Clay Statue is removed from their inventory.

Clay Statue 2

When Clay Statue Questline = 2, a second clay statue event will appear in A Wing 2F. If the player interacts with it, a similar event will appear to the first statue. The player will be given a choice to pick up the statue or not. If they do, Clay Statue Questline = 3 and the item Clay Statue 2 gets added to the player’s inventory. If Clay Statue Questline = 3, A new conversation happens if the player talks with Jillian. At the end, Clay Statue Questline = 4 and the player is transported to B Wing 1F. When loading into the map, because Clay Statue Questline is 4, the JillianDummy event becomes active, which is an autorun event that triggers the following cutscene. Near the end, the player gets the original clay statue back, though since there’s no way to change the name and description of an item, they get a new item called Clay Statue 1. After that, Jillian walks away and Clay Statue Questline = 5.

Clay Statue 3

Similarly to the previous, once Clay Statue Questline = 5, the third clay statue appears, this time in E Wing 3F. The player is once again given the choice to pick it up or not. If they do, Clay Statue 3 will be added to their inventory, and Clay Statue Questline = 6. Heading over to Tim’s locker after this initiates the final cutscene.

Ending

While Clay Statue Questline = 6, if the player interacts with the Tim’s Locker event, a sound effect will play, TimDummy will become active again with Tim’s spritesheet as he moves toward the player. At the end of this interaction, Clay Statue Questline = 7 and the player is transferred to E Wing 1F. When the map loads, because Clay Statue Questline = 7, an autorun event will play. This autorun makes the TimDummy in this map active. At the end, the screen fades out, TimDummy changes images to nothing to imply that he left, then the screen fades back.
