Known Issues - Trucking Release III - as of Monday, Sept 26, 2022

Issue #

Priority

Description

Impact or Workaround

TI-1307

High

Very difficult for student to succeed at the Broken or Missing Clamp inspection step in the engine compartment

Release III does not ever show these clamps in broken or missing state. No user impact.

TI-1265

High

Engine oil check - physical inspection button sometimes plays only part of the animation

If partial animation plays, student should just click the oil check button again and the animation will play correctly from the beginning. This problem occurs very rarely.

TI-1298

Medium

In warm engine check, it asks to inspect engine hoses before warning to listen carefully for air leaks

The hissing air leak is very noticeable, students are very unlikely to miss it even if they aren’t listening carefully.

TI-1292

Medium

Textures for good brake chambers might appear to look cracked

These appear correctly in Release III but could be mistaken under some lighting conditions to look cracked when they are good. Textures will be smoothed and made more obvious in the next release.

TI-1289

Medium

Excessive camera movement in the inspection sim can cause user discomfort

There are areas where camera movement could be made smoother or gentler to enhance user comfort.

TI-1276

Medium

Inspection Checklist does not complete all tasks, even when I have completed Guided Inspection

This is a very minor bug because the checklist is not a priority feature for Guided Inspection ( it is used mainly in Advanced Inspection). Student can safely disregard the task count in this release.

TI-1275

Medium

Both windshield wipers missing is unrealistic and difficult to spot

Minor cosmetic issue.

TI-1208

Medium

The inspection checklist shows the debris for PS and DS engine as completed when only the inspection for PS engine has been completed

The progress checklist is not a priority feature for Guided Inspection. This will be fixed in time to use the checklist with Advanced Inspection.

TI-1312

Low

When loading a simulation and opening the hood, sometimes the latch and the hood open simultaneously

Minor cosmetic issue, no user impact.

TI-1293

Low

Need to fix hood Interior mesh to fix the clipping near the hood latch

Minor cosmetic issue, no user impact.

TI-1308

Low

Tooltip contains typo "Turbercharger" instead of "Turbocharger" in the PS engine compartment

Minor cosmetic issue, no user impact.

TI-1294

Low

When inspecting the wires during the engine warm check, the sparks are highlighted and look strange

Minor cosmetic issue, no user impact.

TI-1288

Low

Task prompt says "left over tools" during the engine inspection, which is hard to understand

Minor cosmetic issue, no user impact.

TI-1114

Low

Annoying air buzzing sound when starting up the sim.

Minor annoying issue, no user impact.

TI-952

Low

Instructors cannot use Save and Load - they do not have a LearnerID from the LMS

This might not be something we can solve, we continue to explore ways to uniquely identify Instructors so that we can enable the Save and Load feature for these users.

TI-1313

Lowest

Sometimes the tutorial intro is skipped when switching between guided mode and tutorial

Minor issue, no user impact.
