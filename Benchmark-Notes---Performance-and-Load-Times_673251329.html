VARLab : Benchmark Notes - Performance and Load Times

Created by Stacey Dineen (Unlicensed), last modified on Apr 12, 2023

I am creating a very informal set of notes to document my own experience of using the Trucking Pre-Trip Inspection DLX at home via eConestoga. I have mid-grade home internet service via a large national-level provider, which I share with my two teenage children. My laptop is a bit above average, but still within the BYOD specifications for the Trucking program. Here are some of my observations:

For anyone not interested in the details, my anecdotal testing showed a shocking range of load times.

Fastest: 154 seconds (2m 34s)

Slowest: 566 seconds (9m 26s)

Not surprisingly, variation in my home internet download speed was the biggest factor in load time performance. This range is so broad that the ‘Average’ times shown below are not meaningful.
