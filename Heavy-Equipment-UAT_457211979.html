https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-398

No effect on the end user - this is a QoL issue for developers.

MINOR IMPACT OCCURS RARELY

Damage decal can still be placed in such a manner that it is visible on this object.

3

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-422

No ability to freely explore the backhoe at the completion of the pre-trip inspection.

MINOR IMPACT

This was not a requirement of the project, the guided inspection is.

4

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-491

The cracked lift arm state will always look exactly the same.

MINOR IMPACT

This was just to add more variety to what the cracked arm state would look like, the existing state is still functional.

5

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-520

Pins around the backhoe might be difficult to see and click.

MINOR IMPACT

The pins are always visible and clickable, satisfying the requirements. They could be improved upon though.

6

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-530

If the learner pans the camera too far to the side, aesthetic decals may disappear right at the edge of the screen.

MINOR IMPACT

Very minor issue, the decals here are just for added detail and not part of any inspection. Appears to be related to the way unity renders objects, at least in our sim.

7

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-536

Learner will inspect external visibility lights (Front, Rear, Top) at a different point than the left and right indicator lights.

MINOR IMPACT

Minor issue, doesn’t impact learner’s progress or ability to inspect any of the lights. More so QoL.

8

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-555

If issue occurs, learner will be unable to progress to the opening hood task.

CANNOT REPRODUCE OCCURS RARELY

Unable to reproduce issue, only reported once.

9

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-561

Potential for learner to get stuck at swing linkage pin inspection and unable to progress

CANNOT REPRODUCE OCCURS RARELY

Unable to reproduce issue, only reported once.

10

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-563

Learner may be unable to see the component they are trying to inspect, possibly causing them to get stuck if they do not click off to close the inspection window and retry.

MINOR IMPACT WORKAROUND AVAILABLE

Relatively minor issue, the learner only has to close the inspection window to see the component fully.

11

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-573

Learner will be unable to see the previous toast message on screen if they have performed an action to cause another to appear.

MINOR IMPACT

The most important and relevant message the learner will want to see is the latest toast message. It will always be on the top layer and visible.

12

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-577

When clicking the key to put the engine into run or start, the key doesn’t actually rotate.

MINOR IMPACT

This is only a visual change, no impact on the functionality of the sim is impacted.

13

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-579

When refilling the fuel tank, the learner will see the jerry can and fuel tank cap slightly clip into the backhoe body.

MINOR IMPACT

Minor visual issue, doesn’t impact progress or the completion of the task.

14

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-587

Learner will not have an opportunity to change their selections after inspecting everything in the current checklist.

WORKAROUND AVAILABLE

Learner is able to inspect and perform every task required, as the sim is purely for learning purposes no grade is assigned for incorrect answers. The learner can replay the sim as much as they want.

15

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-590

If the learner pans the camera to the side to far the oil on the dipstick will disappear, but only at the very edge of the screen.

MINOR IMPACT

Very minor issue, if the learner is viewing the oil in the normal screen space it will be completely visible and inspectable. Similar to the https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-530 issue.

16

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-595

Extremely rare issue that has only been reported once. If it does occur the learner would have difficulty reading the component being inspected.

CANNOT REPRODUCE OCCURS RARELY

Unable to reproduce bug, no one other than the original reporter has experienced the same problem.

17

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-608

Learner will have to click directly on each pin.

MINOR IMPACT

Pins are clickable, the learner just has to make sure to click on them directly. Potentially increasing collider size could be an improvement.

18

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-615

Learner can still inspect just by clicking on the component and choosing the state from the pop-up inspection window.

MINOR IMPACT

Issue doesn’t stop learner from progressing, still inspectable via clicking on the component directly or opening the checklist manually.

19

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-628

A tooltip will appear in the upper right hand corner, this won’t hinder them in any way from inspecting.

MINOR IMPACT

Only a minor visual annoyance, doesn’t impact the learner’s progress.

20

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-629

Learner may not know what a component is by simply hovering the mouse over it. Will have to click it when it comes time to inspect to determine what it is.

MINOR IMPACT

Tooltips are an additional help to the learner but aren’t required for them to inspect any component.

21

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-633

Potential crash while running the sim.

CANNOT REPRODUCE OCCURS RARELY

Was unable to reliably reproduce issue.

FOUND AFTER UAT BUILD DEPLOYED MINOR IMPACT

Learner is still able to inspect both components, just needs to open the checklist.

24

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-667

Camera POI will be somewhat far away for inspecting the boom latch to inspect it clearly.

FOUND AFTER UAT BUILD DEPLOYED MINOR IMPACT

Still inspectable, learner can progress.

25

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-668

Cracked windows will look a little off, cracks are still visible and can be inspected.

FOUND AFTER UAT BUILD DEPLOYED MINOR IMPACT

Still inspectable, somewhat minor issue

26

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-669

May be unable to accurately inspect every component on the rear arm or notice components.

FOUND AFTER UAT BUILD DEPLOYED

All components are still visible and inspectable, but improvements could be made.

27

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-672

Different term used at this single point could possibly confuse the learner.

FOUND AFTER UAT BUILD DEPLOYED MINOR IMPACT

Was a minor issue that didn’t prevent the learner progressing.

28

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-673

Window in the lower right door is missing a piece when viewed from the inside, but it isn’t missing when viewing from outside. Might confuse the learner and second guess the window state.

FOUND AFTER UAT BUILD DEPLOYED MINOR IMPACT

The windows would have already been inspected by that point, so the missing piece won’t affect their inspection in any way. Additionally, this only happens during the cracked state, which lines up with the illusion that the missing section is a broken window.
This is to do with https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-668, and the changes to the crack material caused this.

29

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-674

Determining the oil levels just by looking at the oil may be difficult.

FOUND AFTER UAT BUILD DEPLOYED WORKAROUND AVAILABLE

A toast message was added onto the oil level to inform the learner of the exact state of the oil.

30

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-675

The learner may be unable to determine the correct state of the pistons.

FOUND AFTER UAT BUILD DEPLOYED

Some work was done to make the damage to the pistons more visible, but it may not have been enough.

31

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-676

The learner might think they missed tasks when in reality these values are the number of correct inspection out of the total number of inspections.

FOUND AFTER UAT BUILD DEPLOYED MINOR IMPACT

Issue wasn’t noticed until after UAT started.

32

https://varlab-dev.atlassian.net/browse/HE-677
