VARLab : Design Pillars

Created by Germán Gaussmann on Sept 26, 2024

This is the foundation of your game and should never change. Your whole design should tie back to these pillars.

Player Agency

The feeling that their interactions in game affect the outcome.

Learn by doing

There is no handholding, the player learns on their own with the feedback provided throughout the experience.

Capitalism Silliness

Light hearted humour based on the pressures of working in a capitalist system. It’s not about “Fighting the Man” but about doing your best while having someone breathing down your neck.
