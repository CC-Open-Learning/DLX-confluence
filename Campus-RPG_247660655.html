VARLab : Campus RPG

Created by Kim Nutter, last modified on Jun 09, 2021

Campus RPG is a 2D wayfinding game to help familiarize new students to Conestoga College. It is the team’s believe that this product will be of value for new and current students and assist with wayfinding on campus. This product will also guide students through the orientation process using in game quests to help them through the on-boarding process. This visual experience will help supplement traditional college campus tours and help alleviate anxiety for new domestic and international students.
