This test plan for PHI outlines the strategy, objectives, resources and schedule for the testing activities. This document is designed to ensure that all functional and non-functional requirements of the DLX are thoroughly tested to meet the quality standards. 

Since the Milestone build was released on 13 Sep 2024 this GR Test Plan will focus primarily on the newly implemented features and workflows, since the milestone build PHI-24F1.6 - 12SEP2024.zip. In order to effectively test the new features, lots of general playthrough testing will be required, in order to generate adequate inspection results and images for the focused testing. So, we will get end to end coverage of the DLX, with a strong focus on the newly implemented features that were not included in the Milestone release.

OBJECTIVE

  • To verify the functions as intended 

  • To identify and document any risks and defects 

  • To provide a basis in decision-making of the DLX release 

TESTING SCOPE 

The following features will be the focus of this test plan:

  1. General inspection workflow - There have been only a small number of new inspectables or changes implemented, so we will specifically list those in the test plan, all other inspectables are available at the discretion of the tester. Some tests require more inspections than others. 

  2. End Inspection workflows - This is a main focus of GR testing, as this feature did not exist in the milestone release. Various workflows, including cancel and resume inspection, will be covered.

  3. In-Game Inspection Results - This is a main focus of GR testing, as this feature did not exist in the milestone release. We will validate the contents of the in-game inspection results compared to Inspection Log, Activity Log, and Gallery shown in the Review panel prior to End Inspection. We will also compare the in-game inspection results displayed in the DLX to the contents of the downloaded Inspection Results to ensure nothing is being cut off or otherwise does not match the in-game results.

  4. Save & Load - This is regression testing with specific emphasis on learner sessions that include Start from Beginning (this does not get a lot of coverage in the milestone build - it is not popular with students) and Save & Load after End Inspection (including cancel End Inspection)

  5. Downloadable Results - This is a main focus of GR testing, as this feature did not exist in the milestone release. Testing will validate the download workflow, including the reminder prompt, contents of the download, and usability of the download UI.

Discard Photo workflow

Perform a visual inspection and take a photo. Select a different inspection tool without marking the photo as Compliant or Non-Compliant. Confirm that you are able to discard the photo.

Take a photo and mark it as Compliant. Return to the same item and inspect it again, take a new photo, and mark it as Compliant again.

Confirm all of the following points:

  • A warning is shown to let you know you previously inspected this item.

  • You can discard the new photo and keep the original photo in the Gallery.

  • You can close the inspection panel and move on to inspect another item.

End Inspection

Cancel End Inspection

In the confirmation message, select to Cancel and return to inspect more items AND update/delete inspections you did previously.

Return to the Review panel and take updated screenshots of your Inspection Log, Activity Log, and Gallery. Keep these for comparison later.

Download Reminder

Move your mouse cursor outside of the playback window in your browser tab before downloading your results. Confirm that the reminder prompt to download results is shown.

Confirm that the prompt is ONLY shown if the mouse cursor is moved outside of the playback window AFTER ending the inspection but BEFORE downloading the results. Try other combinations to ensure this reminder prompt is not being shown at inappropriate times, such as BEFORE ending the inspection or AFTER downloading results.

Downloaded Images - zip file(s)

Verify that the total number of exported images in the downloaded zip file(s) is identical to the number of photos in the Gallery. You may have up to 3 zip files, with 150 images in each zip. Check the contents carefully against your Gallery screenshots from your earlier testing.

Confirm that only your FINAL screenshots match your downloaded results. Ensure that images that were deleted from the Gallery prior to selecting End Inspection and View Summary are NOT included in your downloaded results.

PHI-1324 - GR Test Plan: Downloaded Images - zip file(s) Done

Downloaded Results - verify PDF contents

Use the screenshots that you took earlier in testing to compare the Inspection Log, Activity Log, and all summary information from your downloaded PDF.

Ensure that your downloaded PDF contents match your FINAL screenshots, and not the interim screenshots that you created before adding/changing your inspection log.

PHI-1325 - GR Test Plan: Downloaded Results - verify PDF contents. Done

Downloaded Results - Max download size

Create an inspection log that includes 1 photo of every inspectable item in the entire DLX (total 354 photos). End Inspection and review the summary, then download results.

  • Confirm that all 354 photos are downloaded. Each zip archive will contain up to 150 image files, so there will be multiple zip files. The total number of photos in all zip files must add up to 354.

  • Confirm that the 354 photos are correct - there are no duplicate files (ie. you did not get the same first 150 images in every zip)

  • Repeat test with several different popular web browsers, such as Chrome, Edge, and Firefox

PHI-1328 - GR Test Plan: Downloaded Results - Max download size Done

Downloaded Results - download failure - disk space

Confirm that the download will fail gracefully when there is not enough disk space for the download.

Configure your web browser to save to a very space-restricted location on your local machine (e.g. thumb drive)

End Inspection and proceed to download results and photos - report what happens.

NOTE: This test is designed to fail - it will not be possible to download results when inadequate disk space is available. The purpose of this test is to document what happens in these circumstances.

PHI-1329 - GR Test Plan: Downloaded Results - download failure, disk space. Done

Downloaded Results - download failure - network connectivity issues

Confirm that the download will fail gracefully when there is an interruption in network connectivity during the download.

Right after starting your results download, interrupt your internet connection. You could do this by toggling the wifi setting for your device, causing it to lose/regain/lose network connection. Try different timing intervals, to see how long you can be offline before the dowload will fail. Try different web browsers.

NOTE: This test is designed to fail - offline use of this DLX is not supported. We are testing to document what happens in these circumstaces.

PHI-1330 - GR Test Plan: Downloaded Results - download failure, network connectivity issues Done
