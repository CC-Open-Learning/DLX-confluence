Wayfinding Quest

Purpose

Familiarize students with the school’s layout and promote the ideals of the College (wellness, community, rights and responsibilities).

Method

Introduce a questline which visits key locations where players can engage in an encounter which helps them to progress and take them to new locations. Offer dialogue options and interactivity that lets the player engage with other characters in meaningful ways.

Outcome

The player will be able to more confidently navigate Doon Campus and recognize where to find facilities, as well as better understand the ideals of the College.

Winter Preparation Quest

Purpose

To prepare international students for the harsh conditions of the Canadian winters.

Method

The player explores certain classrooms on Doon campus to collect “mythical winter gear” that allows them to traverse through “winter encounters”, each representing a different temperature.

Outcome

The student understands the importance of being prepared for the winter; at the same time understanding the intricacies of Conestoga’s classroom layout.

“Lore” Quest (Stretch Goal)

Purpose

To prepare international students for the differences in bus and road etiquette.

Method

The player talks with a specific character and is able to inquire about Canadian rules regarding bus and pedestrian etiquette.

Outcome

The player understands the differences in unwritten rules and regulations surrounding transport.
