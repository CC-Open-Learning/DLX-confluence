This test plan for Respiratory Therapy 1.0 outlines the strategy, objectives, resources and schedule for the testing activities. This document is designed to ensure that all functional and non-functional requirements for the milestone release of the DLX are thoroughly tested to meet the quality standards. 

Inclusion Criteria: Orientation - This is an open exploration experience where students can gain familiarity and confidence by exploring 3D models of RT equipment and supplies. Each item is displayed with its name and a meaningful description of what it is and how it is used, or why it is important in respiratory therapy practice. On-demand audio helps students learn how item names are pronounced.

Exclusion Criteria:

  • Procedural segments such as Setup, Patient Prep, Biopsy Procedure, and Post-Procedure are not included in this release.

  • This DLX is intended to be used on hardware that meets or exceeds the Program Device Requirements defined for the Respiratory Therapy program.

  • Offline use of this DLX is not supported, the DLX requires a functional internet connection to load.

OBJECTIVE

  • To verify the functions as intended 

  • To identify and document any risks and defects 

  • To provide a basis in decision-making of the DLX release
