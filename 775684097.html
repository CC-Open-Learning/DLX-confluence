Labels that identify the equipment (e.g. tell you what is inside the package or container) are essential and must be readable in the DLX

Labels that provide other info (e.g. instructions about how to use or store something) are not essential to our learning objectives.

2023/10/13

The main Confluence page clearly identifies that the 3 areas to explore are exterior, patient bay, cab interior. Are there any screens or screen-like devices that will need to have screens that activate and deactivate (aka turn on and off)

2023/10/13

Student is not required to OPERATE any of the equipment or devices in this DLX. Our main goal is to ensure that each item is recognizable and identifiable - if there is anything about the screen that is used to identify the item, then it should be displayed. The student will never turn it on or off.

Are there specific colors for each paramedic bag related to it’s purpose? (Ex: Red is always a trauma bag?)

2023/10/13

No, the bag colour does not matter. Bags can be the same colour as they are shown in the photos but are not required to be any particular colour.
