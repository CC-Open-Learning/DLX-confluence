Objectives for Motive Power 1.1 Service Update Patch

In mid-Fall 2022, we became aware that many students were struggling to complete the Auto Shop Safety Inspection module of the Motive Power DLS. Specifically, we had numerous reports of students getting “stuck” in areas of the scene where they “couldn’t do anything” (this happens when you manually navigate to an area that does not match your current task in the graph scenario) and a very high percentage of students that were unable to complete the floor jack/axle stand inspection station (this is a result of the complex interactions and overly detailed inspections that were used in that station).

  • Automatic navigation from one task to the next. The student will NOT be required to use the View menu to go from one area to the next in the shop, we will just automatically take them where they need to go.

  • Simplified inspection process for axle stands and hydraulic floor jack:  It will not be required for the student to move the camera in this inspection at all - we will show them what we need them to see by moving the camera automatically for each task. The number of items to inspect will be reduced (e.g. no need to inspect each individual wheel, we will just check that the floor jack rolls or is stuck in place).

  • Simplified inspection process for fire safety equipment and emergency phone:  We are going to reduce the total number of fire safety items that need to be clicked on and focus on the most important items (extinguishers and alarm pulls). Student feedback indicates that the emergency phone is hard to see/find, so we are going to add a prompt and optimize the camera view of that item.

Secondary (Non-Critical but Nice-to-Have) Objectives

  • Improved load time and performance This enhancement would apply to both modules of Motive Power 1.0, the Daily Hoist Inspection and Auto Shop Safety Inspection.

  • Better lighting - some items were difficult to see because of shadows or dim lighting. This enhancement would apply to both modules of Motive Power.

  • Shop hazards will now be treated just like other items that need to be inspected, the student will get a prompt to tell them to look around for any hazards before they leave a particular area of the shop.  They will not be allowed to move on until they identify and clear the hazard in that area (e.g. clutter, tripping hazard, spill, etc)

  • General bug fixing based on student/instructor feedback to date.
