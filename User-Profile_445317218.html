VARLab : User Profile

Created by Hannah Schmitting (Unlicensed), last modified on Mar 28, 2024

The User Profile is much like a user profile anywhere else on the internet; it’s a place to display information about yourself as a creator, customize the look to suit your brand, and in this case, display the games, assets, comics, etc. that you’ve worked on. On itch.io, this is your “landing page”.

By default, your published games will not appear on your profile until you unhide them in the sidebar.

When uploading a banner or background image, there are two orientation options: fixed width and full width. Fixed width keeps the banner at a fixed width of 960px, whereas full width spans across the entire top of the screen.
