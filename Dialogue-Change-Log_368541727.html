CHANGE LOG AUGUST 23 2021

TUTORIAL

>> Welcome to the Conestoga Doon Campus RPG!

>> But first, a quick tutorial to help you on your way.

>> You’ve probably noticed that you can advance dialogue by pressing the left mouse button or the spacebar.

>> These two buttons can also be used to interact with characters and objects.

>> You can use the arrow keys to move, or you can click the spot you want to move to with your mouse.

>> Try interacting with the door at the top of the room.

>> Well done!

>> If you want to interact with a character, click on them with your left mouse button or press the spacebar while standing in front of them.

>> But what if the character is behind a desk? If you want to interact with them, click the blue bell on the desk or press the spacebar while standing in front of it. Give it a try.

>> (Character dialogue)

>> Congratulations, you’ve completed the tutorial! Interact with the door above to begin the game.

Tutorial Transcript:

Hello there, welcome to the Conestoga Doon Campus RPG.

Who am I? That doesn’t matter. What you need to know is how to traverse and make your way through Conestoga.

As you have probably realized, you can advance dialogue by pressing either the left mouse button or the spacebar.

These two buttons are used to interact with anything and everything.

Try interacting with the door at the top of the room.

To move, use either the arrow keys on your keyboard, or click the spot with your mouse.

Well done.

Now, onto interacting with other characters.

Once again, to interact with someone, either click on them with your left mouse button, or the spacebar.

However, in this case, there is a desk in between you and the person. In cases like these, you will need to interact with them on the other side of the desk.

For those using the left mouse button, it’ll mean clicking the space in front of where they’re facing.

For this character, you can either use your spacebar in front of the desk, or click on the spot to the right of the person. Try it out.

Hello. How are you doing?

Congratulations on completing the tutorial. Now, just interact with the door above you to begin the game.
