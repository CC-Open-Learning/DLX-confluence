General Notes  

  

Things to think about  

 

  Characters   

  • C1 Name:  Fanny 

First Scene - Your Personal Equipment 

  • Scene Background: The kitchen (360).  

  • Scene Characters: Fanny

  • Scene Medias:

    • Model Chef (multiple pictures of it)

    • Black Safety Shoes (Laced, Non-marking)

    • Black and White Checked Chefs Pants

    • White Conestoga Jacket

    • Blue Apron & Neckerchief

    • Two Side Towels (White preferable)

    • Chefs toque with Conestoga Logo

    • Knife Kit

    • Baking Kit

    • Pen

    • Sharpie

    • Thermometer

  • Premise: Inform the students about the basic requirements to attend a class at the kitchen. 

Scene Outline  

  • Explain that it is necessary to wear the Black Safety Shoes  (Laced), Black and White Checked Chefs Pants, White Conestoga Jacket, Blue Apron & Neckerchief, Two Side Towels, Chefs toque with Conestoga Logo, Knife Kit and Baking Kit. 

  • Avatar will be explaining and images will be appearing on the screen. 

  • Explain that besides those, 3 small items are also very important (Pen, Sharpie and Thermometer). 

  • Show a picture where the model looks perfect. 

Second Scene - Spot the errors & Quiz 

  • Scene Background: Bloom Kitchen (360 locked) 

  • Scene Characters: Fanny

  • Scene Medias: Different images of the chef model 

  • Premise: A quick game where the user will be able to spot the mistakes of the Chef 

Scene Outline 

  • Point and Click game where the student will have to spot the different errors in the model outfit. 

  • Quiz containing questions about the content saw in the module
