VARLab : Powerline Testing_Module 2A & 2B

Created by Sana Javeed, last modified on Apr 24, 2024

Use this space to report any issues that you may encounter while testing.

Date: April 17, 2024

Volunteers: 8 VARLab members

Total Testing time: 18 hours

Executive Summary

Issues being identified from the testing are as follows:

  • Frequent crashes result from actions such as pressing the Escape key or interacting with levers and outriggers.

  • Operational glitches, such as malfunctioning outriggers and inoperable wheel chocks, have also been identified.

  • Issues arise when the simulation is NOT played in full-screen mode, with functions becoming non-interactable or freezing during certain operations.

  • Inconsistencies in instructions, unclear visual cues, and intermittent functionalities, like the Back button in the controls panel

The issues have been grouped under categories namely:

  • DLX crashes/Freezes

  • Screen resolution

  • Incoherent instructions

  • Operational issues

  • Asset issues and

  • User discomfort/ functional barriers.
