SharePoint UAT Feedback Forms - Bloom

  • Chosen method for gathering feedback: MS Forms

    • This sim is too large to assign all modules to a single group of UAT volunteers

    • We will divide the sim into chunks that can be played & reviewed in approx 30-60 minutes, with one feedback survey at the end of that session that will ask for feedback about all modules in that “chunk”

      • Survey 1 - Bloom UAT Modules 1-4 (Bloom Orientation, Personal Equipment, Food Safety and Labelling, Basics of Sanitation)

      • Survey 2 - Bloom UAT Modules 5-7 (Small Appliances Part 1, 2, and 3)

      • Survey 3 - Bloom UAT Modules 8-10 (Garde Manger, Entremetier, Grill)

      • Survey 4 - Bloom UAT Modules 11-13 (Pastry, Expo, Kitchen Stewardship)

    • We will embed the links to the UAT Feedback Forms into the VTS simulation itself, so that the volunteers cannot exit the simulation without navigating to the feedback form.

    • Our target is 3-5 volunteers per “chunk”
