Students encounter different scenarios that have the possibility to escalate into a larger conflict. Understanding choice of words, body language and strategies people will use to try to control a situation, students learn how to avoid escalating a potential conflict. (Version 2 is an upgrade from version 1 in that it uses voice actors).
