Overview

This document is intended as a guide to establish and maintain the visual language of the Campus RPG project, with particular focus on environmental assets and UI.

The mission of the Campus RPG project’s visual style is to create an engaging, classic pixel RPG experience for current and potential students of Conestoga College, with care taken to represent College spaces in a way that, while not strictly literal, is easily recognizable.

User Interface

UI design for the Campus RPG project should be recognizable as aesthetically similar to classic pixel RPG UI designs, but updated for ease of use.

Environment

The environmental design of the game will heavily reference the real-life architecture and surrounds of Conestoga College’s Doon campus. College spaces will not be to scale - the map would be far too big - and artistic liberties may be taken with regards to the colour, lighting, and placement of objects in some areas to assist gameplay and visual legibility. Environments must still be recognizable as real spaces within the College.
