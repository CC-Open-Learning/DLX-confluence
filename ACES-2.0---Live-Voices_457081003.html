This project was proposed shortly after the release of ACES 1.0 in June, but no traction was gained until voice actors were arranged through Sandra Memmelo of the eLearning Centre through Lisa Trimble.

Objective

There are no plans at this time to modify the content or learning objectives for the ACES course. This release will be focused on replacing the synthetic voice clips with live actor voice clips.

Challenges

  • Working remotely makes it difficult to organize and provide direction to voice actors. Our previous experiments with recording just one character voice at a time proved less than ideal, because there is no conversational flow - every line sounds like a separate thing and the characters do not seem to be relating to each other in a realistic way.

  • The speak blocks and texts (many of which have voice components) in ACES are not collected together in one script, making it difficult to provide actors with a cohesive script.
