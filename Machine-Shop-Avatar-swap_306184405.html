VARLab : Machine Shop Avatar swap

Created by Rounuk Kumaran, last modified on Jul 02, 2021

For our Machine Shop training simulation, we currently don’t have an avatar that adequately represents the proper safety precautions we’d like to teach our students.

To reduce the work necessary to create an avatar for the Machine Shop simulation, we propose taking the head from one of the existing avatars, swapping it to the body of another avatar, and adding additional safety equipment as needed.

We would like to swap to a body with a short-sleeved shirt as that is one of the safety precautions we want to teach students, long-sleeves are considered a safety hazard.
