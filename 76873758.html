Type of Solution

Encounter (Flatscreen)

(PoV) (Guided by Avatar Digital Teaching Assistant overlay on 360 photos of various ‘real life’ environments with interactions with avatars)

Students encounter different scenarios that have the possibility to escalate into a conflict between involved parties. Understanding choice of words, body language and strategies people will use to try to control a situation, students learn how to avoid escalating a potential conflict. (Version 1 differs from Version 2 as the voicing is computer generated on this version).
