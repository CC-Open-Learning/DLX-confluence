Synopsis of User Feedback - 5 Responses Total

  • ALL respondents used a laptop/desktop computer.

  • There was a good mix of Novice (3) and Expert/Experienced (2) respondents in the survey.

  • ALL respondents indicated that they had no problem accessing or launching the DLS.

  • A few respondents (2/5) indicated that they did not feel confident using the simulation after completing the tutorial. One respondent indicated were “not totally clear” about what they were supposed to do in the scenario.

  • On the follow-up question about the Tutorial, a few respondents (2/5) indicated that they had the basics, but they weren’t feeling confident.

  • Most respondents (4/5) indicated that they had got stuck when trying to use the measuring tool in the DLS.

  • Most respondents (3/5) indicated they were unable to obtain the measurements and photos that they felt were necessary to achieve the learning objectives for the simulation.

  • In the follow-up question about being stuck, here are some details that were given:

    • “I thought I was stuck behind the yellow Audi for a moment, as none of the movement controls worked, but after about 6 seconds I was unstuck. The movement could be smoother in general, but overall it seems to work.”

    • “Measuring laying down a base line or extending the tape does not work.” “Cannot use the measuring tape at all”

    • “When trying to make a minor site line adjustment etc, the "camera" would move out of range towards, wall, sky etc. Hard to conduct a stable transition or adjustment. The measurement tool was very difficult and frustrating to use. I [couldn’t do more of] the exercises because of the mouse/measuring…”

    • “ …You must begin the measurement, drag it as far as possible, not save it, exit the app, walk the distance, go back into the app, and finish the measurement. A little difficult and hard to understand if you don't have basic knowledge and trial and error with it.”
