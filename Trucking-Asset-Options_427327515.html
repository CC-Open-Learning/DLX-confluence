VARLab : Trucking Asset Options

Created by Jordan Robinson (Unlicensed), last modified on Sept 05, 2021

For trucking, we want to purchase as much of the content as we possibly can, creating models from scratch only when absolutely necessary.

While our first step should be contacting Freightliner, Volvo, Peterbilt, etc to see if they’ll provide us with assets directly, beyond that we will likely be purchasing assets already made.

In this document we’ll focus solely on the assets we can purchase.

Freightliner Models

For engines, we have the option of these:

Detroit DD16

Cummins X12

Cummins X15

Freightliner Cascadia 2017

  • Highpoly of truck and trailer

  • Lowpoly would require work

  • UV Mapped

  • Basic interior detail

Freightliner Cascadia (Unknown Year)

  • Highpoly of truck

  • No lowpoly

  • No UVs

  • Basic interior detail

Freightliner Cascadia (Unknown Year)

  • Highpoly of truck

  • Basic lowpoly

  • UV Mapped

  • Basic interior model

Freightliner Cascadia (Unknown Year)

  • Highpoly of truck

  • No lowpoly

  • No UVs

  • Full interior modelled

Volvo Models

For Volvo models, we have the option to stick in their real-world engine model as it is available for purchase. We can also use a Cummins X12 or X15 like the Freightliner as they also appear to be options on the Volvo.
