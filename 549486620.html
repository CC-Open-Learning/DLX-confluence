(PoV) (3D Models & Assets)

Pre-service firefighters practice their knowledge of established search and rescue procedures in a burning building to determine if there are victims inside and to rescue them safely outside. Participants must be aware of the proper commands, protocols and responses while constantly monitoring their own oxygen health and safety. This version features additional scenarios, additional environments and the instructor can determine where the victims will be and how many will be there. Additional art assets and refinement of the learning objects in the DLX have been developed with feedback from firefighters - Additional environment, advanced features such as the heat map and ability for the instructor to place victims, etc.
