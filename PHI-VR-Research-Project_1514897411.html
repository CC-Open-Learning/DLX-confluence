Project Introduction

The Public Health Inspection (PHI) Simulation has been adapted for exploratory research on the Meta Quest 3 platform. This project extends the original PHI project, focusing on investigating how existing interactions and features can be transitioned to a VR environment.

This research activity was designed as a flexible and experimental exploration rather than aiming to deliver a fully optimized or finalized VR version.
