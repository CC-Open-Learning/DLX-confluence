Synopsis of User Feedback - 4 Responses Total

  • ALL respondents used a laptop/desktop computer.

  • ALL respondents indicated that they had no problem understanding what to wear or not wear in the shop.

  • ALL respondents indicated that they had no problem navigating or completing the learning activities in the DLS.

  • ALL respondents indicated that they found the self-evaluation questions were easy to understand and answer correctly.

  • ALL respondents indicated that they had did not get stuck at any point in the DLS.

  • Most respondents (3/4) indicated they had no suggestions for changes in the DLS. One user suggested that it might be better if learners were not allowed to skip listening to the dialogue boxes.

  • All respondents indicated that they know enough about PPE and individual preparation to feel safe in the machine shop.

  • One respondent suggested that there should be more questions in the self-evaluation quiz.

  • One respondent suggested that we include information about where students should keep their belongings.
