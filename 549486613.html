Procedural - Professional (Flatscreen & VR Headset)

(PoV) (3D Models & Assets)

Students investigate a randomized series of scenarios with criminal actions at a diner and decide which criminal charges are appropriate. They collect and ‘bag’ evidence, write up the report and identify the appropriate charges. New features in this version include additional evidence and more randomized crime scenes, interviewing witnesses and taking statements.

This immersive learning experience transports students into a procedural crime scene investigation set within a diner, where each session presents a unique and randomized scenario. Utilizing a first-person perspective, learners navigate through the environment, employing 3D models and assets to examine the scene, collect evidence, and interact with witnesses. The goal is to meticulously gather and catalog all relevant data, compile a comprehensive report, and deduce the correct criminal charges based on the evidence at hand. This simulation not only sharpens critical thinking and attention to detail but also provides an introductory understanding of crime scene investigation techniques.
