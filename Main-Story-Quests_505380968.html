VARLab : Main Story Quests

Created by Colm Ambler (Unlicensed) on Aug 02, 2022

Name: General Name for the Quest

ID: Id the game uses for the quest log

Objective: A one or two sentence about the goal of the quest.

Events: A list of the main events of the quest.

Alternative Events: If the player can approach the quest using different methods, then they are listed here.

Major Locations: The major locations the player visits in the quest.

Learning Outcomes: What we want the player to get out of playing through the quest, what's the reasoning for the quest existing?

Characters: The characters that appear and have a part in the quest. These do not include random NPCs such as cashiers or the librarian.

Name: School Supplies

ID: itemQuest

Objective: The condor must collect their school supplies in their apartment before taking the bus to go to school.

Events:

  1. The player begins the game.

  2. The player grabs all four of the items across the apartment.

  3. The player leaves the apartment and takes the bus to school.

Major Locations:

  1. Apartment.

  2. Condor Room.

  3. Bus.

Learning Outcomes

  • Learn some of the basic items you need to be successful on the first day of school.

  • Learn the etiquette of bus rides in Canada.

Characters

  • Condor

  • Kaedama

Wayfinding Quest

Name: Finding a Friend

ID: ezraQuest

Objective: Find a friend to explore the campus with and help them find the library.

Events:

  1. Enter the campus through door 3.

  2. Talk with the distressed student (Ezra).

  3. Go into the library.

  4. Use one of the many printers in the library.

  5. Go back to the security desk.

Major Locations:

  1. A Building CSI Services.

  2. B Wing 2F.

  3. Library2.

Learning Outcomes:

  • Learn where the library and security desk are.

  • Understand that you can print off documents using the library's computers and printers.

Characters:

  • Condor

  • Ezra

Winter Quest

Note Most of the Winter Quests are very similar in design so most of the info for these quests are similar to each other.

Name: Winter Quest

ID: winterQuest

Objective: Begin the winter quest and learn the basics of a Canadian Winter.

Events:

  1. Walk towards B Wing and meet Old Man Winter.

  2. Find The Den and walk into the portal.

  3. Attempt to win against Winston the Snowman and fail.

Major Locations:

  1. A Wing 2F.

  2. The Den.

Learning Objectives:

  • Learn the location of The Den.

  • Learn the importance of mittens.

  • Experience the dangers of a harsh Canadian winter, specifically February as mentioned in the scene.

  • Basic information about frostbite and the cold.

Characters:

  • Condor

  • Ezra

  • Old Man Winter

  • Charlie

The End

Name: The Pond

ID: pondQuest

Objective: After completing the winter and wayfinding quest, each character is ready to leave. Before they do, they decide to spend a moment relaxing at the pond.

Events:

  1. Complete the Winter Quest in the Den or the Wayfinding Quest in the Bookstore.

  2. Go towards the Pond

Alternative Events:

  1. The player can begin the quest in two ways, with two cutscenes depending on it.

  2. At the end, the player gets to choose whether they finish the game and see the credits or go back to the school. 

    1. If they do this, Charlie will appear in the winter room.

  3. In the pond scene, quest-related NPCs will appear in the background depending on whether or not the player has completed their corresponding quest.

Major Locations:

  1. The Pond.

  2. Credits.

Learning Objectives:

  • Learn where the pond is.
